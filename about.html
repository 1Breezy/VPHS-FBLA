Villa Park High School FBLA is part of the largest career student organization in the world. Our chapter is dedicated to helping members develop leadership, teamwork, and career skills.

We compete in regional, state, and national events, while also participating in community service and leadership development activities. Our goal is to empower students with the tools they need to succeed in business and beyond.
